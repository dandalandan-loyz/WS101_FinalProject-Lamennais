COMMON QUESTIONS

Frequently Asked Questions

How can I volunteer with the community?
You can volunteer by sending us a message using the form above. Tell us your availability and interests, and we’ll recommend a program where you can help.
What programs are available for young people?
We offer youth ministry activities, retreats, leadership training, and formation programs. Check our Services page for the full list.
How can I learn more about religious life with the Brothers?
Send us a message and choose “Vocation Promotion” as your subject. We’ll schedule an orientation and provide resources for discernment.
