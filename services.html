Our Services & Programs

Discover how we serve the community through education, ministry, and outreach, following the charism of Blessed Jean-Marie de la Mennais.

OUR OFFERINGS

Programs & Ministries

Our community is engaged in various forms of apostolate, all aimed at making Jesus Christ known and loved, especially among the young.

🎓

Education

Quality Christian education from elementary to secondary levels, forming students in knowledge, values, and faith.

👥

Youth Ministry

Dynamic programs for young people including retreats, camps, leadership training, and faith formation activities.

♥

Community Outreach

Reaching out to marginalized communities through feeding programs, medical missions, and livelihood assistance.

📖

Catechesis

Systematic faith formation programs for children, youth, and adults to deepen their understanding of Catholic teachings.

⛪

Parish Ministry

Active collaboration with local parishes in liturgical services, sacramental preparation, and pastoral care.

✨

Retreats & Recollections

Spiritual renewal programs designed to help participants encounter God and deepen their prayer life.

🤝

Volunteer Programs

Opportunities for lay people to participate in our mission through teaching, mentoring, and service projects.

➕

Vocation Promotion

Accompanying young men who are discerning a call to religious life as Brothers of Christian Instruction.

📚

Formation Programs

Comprehensive spiritual and human formation for aspirants, postulants, and novices in religious life.

WHY JOIN US

Meaningful

  • Develop leadership skills through hands-on experience
