WHO WE ARE

A Community of Brothers
United in Mission

The La Mennais Community of San Jose de Buenvista is part of the worldwide congregation of the Brothers of Christian Instruction (FIC), founded by Blessed Jean-Marie de la Mennais in 1819.

Our mission is rooted in the education and evangelization of young people, especially those in rural and underserved areas. We believe in making knowledge accessible to all, following the example of our founder who dedicated his life to Christian education.

In Antique, we continue this legacy through our schools, parish ministries, youth programs, and community outreach initiatives. We are committed to forming young people who are grounded in faith, equipped with knowledge, and ready to serve.
